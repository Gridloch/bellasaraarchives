Bella Sara has added to the stables! Introducing new horses with an extra bit of fantasy to the world of Bella Sara--horses that swim, fly, and do other magical things.

The new cards are available now and sold at stores like Blockbuster, Kmart, Target, Toys "R" Us, and Wal-Mart, as well as local hobby and equestrian stores. Search our Where to Buy section to find a store near you.

To get ready for the new Second Series cards, we have updated the stablefor the fantasy horses. Your swimming or flying horses will need special air and water stables to make them comfortable, and you'll get to do special new things to take care of them. Get ready for a new look for your regular horses, too. We think you're going to love them--even more!
