Hello and welcome, everyone! My name is Sara. My mom invented the Bella Sara™ game because of my big interest in horses. I'm so horse-crazy that my family always tells me I must have been bitten by a mad horse!

I have two horses. Rozolino Sonecá is a 5-year-old Danish Warmblood gelding. Joy is a Connemara pony, 4 years old. Joy will be for my little sister Freja, but I'm teaching her to behave first.

I've always been very interested in horses. Now I work in a shop where we sell riding gear to both professional and amateur riders.

I hope you enjoy the website and the games.

Hugs, Sara

If you would like to ask Sara a question, you can email her at asksara@bellasara.com.
