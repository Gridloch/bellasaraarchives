Complete Bella Sara Merchandise Line Coming to Stores

A full line of Bella Sara merchandise will be rolled out in stores during 2007. Lovely UltraPRO™ card portfolios featuring Bella, Jewel, Fiona, and Thunder are already on sale at many stores where Bella Sara cards are sold. Keep an eye out for other Bella Sara products throughout the year, like Bella Sara back-to-school gear and adorable Bella Sara gift sets your best friends will love to receive (and so will you – hint, hint, Mom!).

Expect to see our merchandise on store shelves by next summer. And remember, if your store doesn’t carry our products, ask them to! As Shaman says, "You are more powerful than you know."
